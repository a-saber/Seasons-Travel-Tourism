# Seasons

Welcome to Seasons, the all-in-one travel app designed to simplify your travel planning and enhance your travel experiences. Whether you need to book a flight, find tourism programs, reserve hotels, or even arrange car and train transportation, Seasons has you covered. This README will guide you through the features of the app and provide instructions on how to get started.

## Features

### Flights
- **Book Flights**: Search and book flights to various cities and countries around the world.
- **Manage Bookings**: View and manage your existing flight bookings.
- **Flight Status**: Check the status of your flights in real-time.

### Tourism Programs
- **Explore Programs**: Discover tourism programs in different cities and countries, including tours, excursions, and more.
- **Book Programs**: Easily book your desired tourism programs, with options to include flights if needed.
- **Personalized Recommendations**: Get personalized recommendations based on your preferences and travel history.

### Hotel Reservations
- **Find Hotels**: Search for hotels in different cities and countries based on your criteria (location, price, rating, etc.).
- **Book Hotels**: Reserve your preferred hotel with just a few taps.
- **Manage Reservations**: View and manage your hotel reservations.

### Transportation
- **Cars**: Book rental cars in various cities and countries.
- **Trains**: Search and book train tickets for domestic and international routes.
- **Manage Transport**: View and manage your car rentals and train bookings.
